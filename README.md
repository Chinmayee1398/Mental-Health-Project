# Features
Happy Fox integrates various UI and UX design principles to provide a seamless and engaging user experience. Key features include:

* üíÜ Relaxation Techniques: The app offers a variety of guided meditations, breathing exercises, and progressive muscle relaxation techniques presented in an intuitive and visually appealing manner, promoting relaxation and stress management.

* üå¨Ô∏è Breathing Exercises: Happy Fox incorporates user-friendly interfaces and smooth animations to guide users through specialized breathing exercises, enhancing the sense of calmness and focus.

* üßò Timed Meditations: The app utilizes gestalt principles to present visually cohesive and immersive guided meditation experiences. Users can select from a range of meditations tailored to specific needs, promoting emotional balance, improved sleep, and self-compassion.

* ‚è∞ Breaks: Happy Fox offers a dedicated "Breaks" feature that reminds users to take care of their physical and mental well-being throughout the day. It includes the following reminders:

  * üíß Water Reminder: Regular hydration is important for overall health. Happy Fox reminds users to drink water at regular intervals, promoting hydration and well-being.    
  * üèãÔ∏è Exercise Reminder: Physical activity is beneficial for mental health. The app sends reminders to encourage users to engage in exercise or physical activity, boosting mood and reducing stress.
  * üåº Relaxing Breaks: Happy Fox suggests short relaxing activities, such as deep breathing exercises or quick mindfulness sessions, to provide moments of calm and rejuvenation during busy schedules.
